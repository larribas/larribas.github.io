About me

I’m Lorenzo Arribas, a Software Engineer based in Barcelona, Spain.

Over the past 7 years, I have worked in 2 hypergrowth start-ups and seen them turn into well-established multi-national companies. I’ve also ventured into creating my own company for a couple of years.

I like fast-paced environments where I can have an impact and feel like every day is challenging. I try not to stay within my comfort zone. During the last 7 years I’ve put frontend applications into production, developed event-driven microservices and set up complex infrastructure topologies.

From time to time, I also post my thoughts and projects on my blog.

Some of my last projects have involved:

  • Migrating a critical backend service to a highly available, distributed system. 1
  • Adopting Continuous Delivery on a monolith with 70+ concurrent engineers and going from 1 to 15 deployments per day.
  • Deploying Machine Learning projects at scale, with 1M online inferences per hour.

I’m always open to hearing about new opportunities or projects. If you think I can be a good fit, don’t hesitate to drop me an email.
